About Us

Who We Are

Uttara University Blood Club (UUBC) is a volunteer-based organization dedicated to saving lives by connecting blood donors with patients in need. We work selflessly to build a strong network of donors and raise awareness about the importance of blood donation.

Our Mission

To ensure that no life is lost due to the unavailability of blood by creating a reliable, active, and responsive donor community at Uttara University.

Our Vision

A society where every individual understands the value of blood donation and comes forward willingly to save lives.

What We Do

  • Maintain a donor database
  • Awareness programs and workshops
